# Blade Ward
*cantrip, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Whenever a creature makes an attack roll against you before the spell ends, the attacker subtracts `d4` from the attack roll.
