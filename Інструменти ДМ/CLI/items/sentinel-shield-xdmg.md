# Sentinel Shield
*Armor ([shield](Інструменти%20ДМ/CLI/items/shield-xphb.md)), uncommon*  


- **Armor Class**: 2
- **Weight**: 6.0 lbs.

While holding this Shield, you have [Advantage](Інструменти%20ДМ/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on [Initiative](Інструменти%20ДМ/CLI/rules/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md) rolls and Wisdom ([Perception](Інструменти%20ДМ/CLI/rules/skills.md#Perception)) checks. The Shield is emblazoned with a symbol of an eye.
